About Me

My name is Xander Povey. I am currently a PhD student at Imperial College London thinking about problems in low dimensional topology using gauge theory—mostly monopole theory—working with Steven Sivek. Additionally, I am a Martingale scholar from the inaugural cohort of 2023. Before, I did an MSc in Pure Mathematics at Imperial College, in which my master's thesis, on Seiberg-Witten invariants, was supervised by Simon Donaldson. In 2023, I finished my undergraduate studies in Mathematics at Trinity College, Cambridge.
